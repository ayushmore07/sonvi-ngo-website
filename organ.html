What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation is the process of donating healthy organs or tissues to save or improve someone elseâ€™s life. A single donor can save multiple lives and give hope to patients suffering from critical illnesses.

Why is Organ Donation Important?

Thousands of patients wait for life-saving transplants every year. Organ donation allows them to live healthy, meaningful lives. It is a noble act that continues life even after death.

Who Can Become a Donor?

Any individual above 18 years of age can register. Final eligibility is determined by medical professionals at the time of donation.

Organs That Can Be Donated

  • Kidney
  • Liver
  • Heart
  • Lungs
  • Eyes (Cornea)
  • Skin

How to Register?

Fill out the registration form on this website, provide accurate details, select the organs you wish to donate, and inform your family about your decision.
